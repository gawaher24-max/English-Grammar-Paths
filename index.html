1. Nominal Sentences (State)

Present: Am/Is/Are
Past: Was/Were
Future: Will be

● Positive: S + [Be] + Complement. (He is happy)

● Negative: S + [Be] + NOT + Complement. (They were not ready)

● Simple Q: [Be] + S + Complement? (Are you okay?)

● Double Q: WH + [Be] + S? (Where will you be?)

2. Verbal Sentences (Action)

Present: Do/Does
Past: Did
Future: Will

● Positive: S + Verb (s/ed/base). (She works hard)

● Negative: S + [Aux] + NOT + Verb. (I don't play)

● Simple Q: [Aux] + S + Verb? (Did you sleep?)

● Double Q: WH + [Aux] + S + Verb? (When will you go?)
