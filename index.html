1. Nominal Sentences (The "Be" Path)

● Positive / Affirmative:

Subject + [Am/Is/Are | Was/Were | Will be] + Adj/Noun.

Ex: She is smart | They will be ready.

● Negative:

Subject + [Be] + NOT + Adj/Noun.

Ex: I am not late | He was not here.

● Simple Question (Yes/No):

[Be] + Subject + Adj/Noun?

Ex: Are you happy? | Will she be there?

● Double Question (WH-):

WH-Word + [Be] + Subject?

Ex: Where are you? | How was the movie?

2. Verbal Path & Tenses

Present

Uses Do/Does for questions and negatives. Singular subjects add -s to verbs.

Past

Uses Did for questions and negatives. Positive sentences use Verb-ed.

Future

Uses Will for all subjects. Very simple: Subject + Will + Verb (base).
