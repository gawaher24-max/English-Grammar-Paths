Full Grammar Map

1. Nominal (Be)

Focuses on status, identity, or location. Uses Am, Is, Are (Present) or Was, Were (Past).

2. Verbal (Action)

Focuses on movements or activities. Uses Base Verb or Verb+s (Present) and Verb+ed (Past).

3. Forms & Structures

  • Positive: Subject + Verb (or Be)
  • Negative: Subject + Auxiliary (Don't/Doesn't/Didn't) + Not
  • Questions: Start with Auxiliary (Do/Does/Did/Be) before the Subject.
  • WH- Questions: WH-Word + Auxiliary + Subject + Verb.
